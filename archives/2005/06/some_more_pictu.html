Some more pictures of Yellowstone

Yellowstone was so interesting that we decided that we would send out some more pictures of it

Hot springs & geysers are all over and in every colour and every consistency, from steam to water to mud pots.

  

The fire of 1988 still is very much in evidence – however, because the new growth of lodge pole pines is only 3 to 8 feet high the vistas can still be seen.

   

And the vistas are wonderful
